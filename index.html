about me

Hi my name is Kendall Roberts, the creator of Humming Your Truth. I started coaching because I am dedicated to the empowerment of women of color, especially Black women. Supporting them with adding resources to their spiritual toolbox, through self discovery, building community, developing strong self-care habits, finding balance, and personal/professional goal setting. I believe that creating strong community amongst women of color supports with their personal healing as well as healing within Black and Brown communities.
Over the past three years I have been dedicated to working with women and girls that have experienced violence. I have facilitated women’s empowerment workshops at transitional housing programs to teen mothers and provided life coaching to women who are survivors of exploitation, as well as youth that are at risk of exploitation. I earned my Bachelors of Arts in Social Work with a minor in African Studies and have recently become a certified Life Coach.

Mission

To empower women of color, especially Black women to recognize the importance of self-preservation through providing them tools for developing healthy coping skills, implementing strong self-care habits, and building confidence.

Values

I believe that creating strong community amongst women of color supports with personal healing, as well as healing within Black and Brown communities.

Work with me

I offer 1:1 in person or telephone/video call coaching sessions to support women with breaking through limiting beliefs, identify the importance of self-preservation, and incorporating self-care into their daily routine to improve quality of life. Sessions will be centered around your personal and professional goals that you have for yourself. I want you to feel heard, safe, and supported through your journey.
I will be offering women’s empowerment workshops focused on supporting women, especially women of color with adding resources to their spiritual toolbox. Teaching them the importance of self-preservation through developing strong self-care habits.
